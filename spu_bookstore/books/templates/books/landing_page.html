About Us

SPU Books is designed to streamline the exchange of second-hand textbooks among SPU students. Specifically, this platform facilitates transactions between senior students (e.g., 2nd year) and junior students (e.g., 1st year), providing a convenient alternative to crowded WhatsApp groups.

Our goal is to make textbook acquisition more affordable and efficient, ensuring that valuable resources are passed down to those who need them.
